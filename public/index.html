How it works

  1. Everyone joins a room and the host starts the draft.
  2. On your turn, 3 random teams are shown — pick 1. The other 2 go back into the pool.
  3. Round robin continues until all 68 teams are claimed.
  4. As the tournament plays out, eliminated teams are tracked automatically.
  5. Last team standing wins the hoodie.
